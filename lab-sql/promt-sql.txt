Every lab must include generate_flag.py and entrypoint.sh.

## generate_flag.py

import random,string
flag='IDS{'+''.join(random.choices(string.ascii_uppercase+string.digits,k=14))+'}'
open('/tmp/flag.txt','w').write(flag)
print(flag)

## entrypoint.sh

#!/bin/bash
sleep 5
python3 /app/generate_flag.py
FLAG=$(cat /tmp/flag.txt)
mysql -h lab_eX_db -uroot -proot lab -e "UPDATE flags SET flag='$FLAG' WHERE id=1;"
apache2-foreground

Replace lab_eX_db per lab.
